High-impact features
1. Treatment recommendations for detected diseases
After a scan, show suggested treatments (organic and chemical) in Cebuano.
Link to common products and application steps.
Use AI to generate recommendations from the detected disease.
2. Field management
Add/edit/delete rice fields with name, area, planting date, variety.
Associate scans with specific fields.
Simple growth stage tracking (e.g., vegetative, reproductive, ripening).
Optional yield logging per field.
3. Disease alerts
Implement the existing “Disease Alerts” toggle.
Use location + weather + scan history to estimate risk.
Push notifications when risk is high in the user’s area.
4. Weather alerts
Implement the existing “Weather Alerts” toggle.
Notifications for heavy rain, drought risk, strong winds, typhoons.
Use the current weather API and location.
5. Offline local disease model
Integrate a small on-device model (e.g., TensorFlow Lite) for disease detection when offline.
Fallback when there’s no internet, as mentioned in the scan flow.
Medium-impact features
6. Rice variety database
List common Philippine rice varieties (e.g., IR64, NSIC Rc222).
Basic info: growth duration, resistance, best practices.
AI assistant can reference this when giving advice.
7. Pest identification
Extend scanning to pests (e.g., rice bugs, stem borers).
Similar flow to leaf disease scanning.
Simple pest–control suggestions.
8. Irrigation / water schedule
Simple irrigation reminders based on growth stage and weather.
Optional water level tracking for fields.
9. Harvest calendar
Estimate harvest dates from planting date and variety.
Reminders for pre-harvest tasks (e.g., drying, storage prep).
10. Export / share scan reports
Export scan results as PDF or image.
Share with extension workers or buyers.
Optional print-friendly layout.
Nice-to-have features
11. Multi-language
Support Tagalog and English in addition to Cebuano.
Language setting in Settings.
12. Community / knowledge base
FAQ or short articles on common diseases and practices.
Searchable in Cebuano.
13. Price / market info
Basic palay price trends (if data is available).
Optional links to DA or local market info.
14. Photo gallery per field
Attach photos to fields over time.
Track crop progress and issues.
15. Backup and sync
Cloud backup of scan history and field data.
Restore on new device.
Quick wins (low effort)
Connect Settings feature tiles – Make Scan & Analyze, Weather, AI Assistant navigate to the right screens.
Pull-to-refresh on Scan History – Refresh the list.
Search in AI chat – Search past conversations.
Favorites / bookmarks – Save important AI answers.
Onboarding tips – Short tutorial on first launch.
Suggested order
Connect Settings feature tiles.
Treatment recommendations for scan results.
Field management (add/edit fields, link scans).
Disease and weather alerts.
Offline local model when ready.
These build on your existing scan, weather, AI, and field models and fit the app’s purpose for local farmers.
